🎯 Critical Success Factor

The breakthrough came from recognizing that 70% automation required classifying ALL procurement spend (IT + non-IT), not just IT spend. This scope expansion, combined with comprehensive commodity mapping, unlocked 93% automation.

Key Learnings

1. Understand your data before setting targets - We initially targeted IT-only classification, missing 50% of the data.

2. Leverage existing taxonomies - Using SC/BS codes from the spreadsheet saved weeks of work.

3. Focus on high-leverage stages - 87% of success came from just two stages (commodity + exact match).

4. Iterate with external critique - Gemini validation identified the scope constraint that we'd missed.

5. Know when to stop - 93% is excellent. The remaining 4% are legitimately edge cases.
