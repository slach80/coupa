Problem Statement

The current manual process for assigning activity codes to Coupa invoices creates significant operational burden and delays in financial reporting:

Invoice lines processed per month

Business Impact

  • Delayed Financial Reporting: Incomplete activity codes prevent accurate spend analytics and budget tracking
  • Resource Drain: Finance team spends months on repetitive classification work instead of strategic analysis
  • Inconsistency: Manual decisions vary across team members, creating classification inconsistencies
  • Scalability Issues: Growing invoice volume makes manual process unsustainable
  • Audit Risk: Lack of documented rationale for classification decisions

Solution Value Proposition

Transform Months into Hours

Automated classification system processes 15,000+ invoice lines in minutes with 95%+ accuracy, enabling real-time spend visibility and freeing finance team for strategic work.

Core Benefits

⚡ Speed & Efficiency

Reduce classification time from months to hours (95%+ time savings). Process entire quarters in a single batch run.

🎯 Accuracy & Consistency

Rule-based AI classification ensures consistent decisions. Target 95%+ accuracy with confidence scoring to flag uncertain cases.

📊 Real-Time Insights

Enable immediate spend analytics, IT budget tracking, and vendor performance monitoring without waiting for manual classification.

🔄 Continuous Learning

System learns from manual corrections via feedback loop. Accuracy improves over time as mapping database grows.

📋 Audit Trail

Complete history of classification decisions, confidence scores, and rationale. Supports compliance and quality review.

📈 Scalability

Handles invoice volume growth without additional headcount. System performance scales linearly.
